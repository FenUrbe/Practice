Pizza

Making pizza at home is easy and fun! With simple ingredients like dough, sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings, you can create a delicious pizza in no time. It’s a great way to enjoy a homemade meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.)
  • Olive oil (optional, for brushing the crust)

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Prepare Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Add Sauce: Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Add Cheese: Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Add Toppings: Place your favorite toppings on top of the cheese.
  6. Bake: Transfer the pizza to a baking sheet or pizza stone and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Optional Step: Brush the crust with olive oil for a golden, crispy finish.
  8. Serve: Remove from the oven and let it cool for a minute before slicing and serving.
